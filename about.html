WHO ARE WE

The Keyboard Warrior Community is an organization that advocates for the use of cyberbullying and raises awareness of the importance of pointing out others’ fault and implementing justice in the online community. The term “keyboard warrior” refers to a person who behaves aggressively or in an inflammatory manner in an online text-based discussion but at the same time does not behave similarly in real life, potentially due to cowardice, introversion or shyness.

HOW TO JOIN US

WHAT WE DO

This online community of keyboard warriors was created and established to provide a safe haven and supportive atmosphere for all who identifies themselves as keyboard warriors. Together, on this online platform, we are able to unite and find others who share the same righteous goal as us. In all, we hope to educate and promote the beliefs of our members.
